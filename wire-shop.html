Wireless Workshop

Join us for a one-day event focused on teaching networking and wireless hacking. Bring your laptop and learn how to sniff traffic, crack WEP encyrption, deauth users, spoofing and much more! The event will cost $30 for non-ACM members or $25 for ACM members. This includes hacking hardware tools (wireless card, Kali live boot) and a how-to manual that you can take home!
The event requires no previous experience and will assume you know nothing about the hardware or the tools we will be using. Registration includes a pizza dinner.
