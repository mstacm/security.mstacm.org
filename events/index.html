Wireless Workshop

Join us for a one-day event focused on teaching networking and wireless hacking. Bring your laptop and learn how to:

  • Sniff network traffic
  • Crack Wi-Fi encryption
  • De-auth devices
  • Spoof access points
  • And much more!

This includes hacking hardware tools (wireless card, LanTap Ninja Star) and a how-to manual that you can take home! The event requires no previous experience and will assume you know nothing about the hardware or the tools we will be using.

Registration closes Thursday, Sept. 24th at 11:59 PM.
Limited to 20 people.
