Cyber Boot Camp

Cyber Boot Camp is a annual training camp held at both Sandia and Los Alamos National Laboratories (Albuquerque, New Mexico and Los Alamos, New Mexico). This year we will be hosting this event virtually and in-person.

Here we are taught in a hands-on environment about topics such as:
  • Host forensics
  • Cryptography
  • Reverse engineering
  • Dev-ops
  • Operational red-teaming
  • Malware analysis
  • Network infrastructure security
  • And more!

Get a chance to be taught by dedicated professionals and increase your experience and networking! We will be holding two days of instruction from each lab.

Event goes January 9th - 15th, 2022.
Registration closes at midnight on October 29.
Limited to 15 participants in-person and 15 digitally.
