Wireless Workshop

Join us for a one-day event focused on teaching networking and wireless hacking. Bring your laptop and learn how to:

  • Sniff network traffic
  • Crack Wi-Fi encryption
  • De-auth devices
  • Spoof access points
  • And more!

This event includes a long-range USB Wi-Fi adapter that you can take home! Requires no previous experience and will assume no prior knowledge about the hardware or the tools we will be using.
