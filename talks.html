Wireless Workshop Competition

By ACM SIG-Security

CS Building 207, October 28 2018, 2:00pm - 6:00pm

Description: The Wireless Workshop competition is a one-day event focused on teaching networking and wireless hacking. Bring your laptop and learn how to sniff traffic, crack WEP encyrption, deauth users, and play with cool hacking tools and toys. The event will cost $25 for non-ACM members or $20 for ACM members. This can include hacking hardware tools (wireless card, Kali live boot) and a how-to manual that you can take home! For admission only without the hardware it is $15 for non-ACM members and $10 for ACM members.
