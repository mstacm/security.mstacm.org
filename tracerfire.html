Tracer FIRE Workshop and Competition

ACM SIG-Security is proud to host a Tracer FIRE (Forensic Incident Response Exercise) weekend August 25th - 27th at Missouri S&T by Sandia National Laboratories. Tracer FIRE is a program developed by Sandia and Los Alamos National Laboratories to educate and train cyber-security incident responders, analysts, and university students, in critical skill areas including incident response, forensic investigation and analysis, file systems, memory layout and malware analysis.
