Red vs. Blue

Date: Tuesday, March 5, 2013

Time: 7:00pm

Place: CS207

Abstract: This network intrusion talk will show both sides of a cyber attack. The presentation will be given by Ryan Birmingham, a Sandia National Labs red teaming member, as well as the Missouri S&T Cyber Defense Team, who recently competed in the Missouri State Cyber Defense Competition. Come and see how to both attack and defend a network from a live attack.
