Sandia National Laboratories - Center for Cyber Defenders

Date: Tuesday, August 28, 2012

Time: 7:00-8:00pm

Place: CS207

Abstract: In 2000, Sandia National Laboratories began an exciting internship program for students interested in computer security. The Center for Cyber Defenders (CCD) has summer and year-round opportunities for students to develop real world experience and expand their knowledge by working on real Sandia projects. Join us for presentations on intern projects from this past summer and learn more about the CCD intern program.
